You gon' get this rain like it's May weather,

from Big Sean (Ft. Jay Electronica & Kendrick Lamar) – Control Lyrics on Rap Genius

Meaning

Rain is a pretty common occurrence in the month of May. But the line also includes some wordplay where rain refers to making it rain, like Floyd “Money” Mayweather. Floyd is the highest paid athlete in the world, so he sure can make it rain.

Making it rain refers to the act of throwing wads of cash in the air so that the bills rain down, normally onto a stripper.
